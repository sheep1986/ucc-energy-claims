About Utility Commission Claims

UK's most trusted energy broker commission recovery specialists. Founded by industry experts with a mission to return what's rightfully yours.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by former energy industry executives, Utility Commission Claims was born from a simple observation: UK businesses were unknowingly paying billions in undisclosed broker commissions on their energy contracts.

Our founders, with over 15 years of combined experience in energy procurement and regulation, recognized that while these hidden charges were perfectly legal to recover, businesses needed expert guidance to navigate the complex claims process.

Since our founding, we've successfully recovered over £1.2 million for 250+ UK businesses, establishing ourselves as the country's premier energy commission claims specialists.
