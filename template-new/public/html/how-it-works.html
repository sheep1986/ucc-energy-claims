Our 5-Step Claims Process

From initial assessment to final payment, we guide you through every step with complete transparency and no upfront costs.

1

Free Initial Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive review of your energy contracts and billing history to identify potential hidden commissions.

What We Do:
  • Review your energy contracts
  • Analyze billing patterns
  • Calculate potential recovery
  • Provide initial estimate
Timeline:

24-48 hours

What You Need:

Recent energy bills and contract documents (we can help obtain these)

2

Legal Documentation & Analysis

Our legal experts conduct in-depth analysis and prepare comprehensive documentation to support your claim.

Legal Review Includes:
  • Contract compliance assessment
  • Regulatory breach identification
  • Evidence compilation
  • Claim value calculation
Timeline:

3-5 business days

Regulatory Basis:

Claims based on Ofgem regulations and consumer protection law

3

Formal Claim Submission

We submit your professionally prepared claim directly to the energy supplier with full legal backing.

Submission Package:
  • Formal demand letter
  • Supporting evidence
  • Legal justification
  • Recovery calculation
Timeline:

1-2 business days

Response Period:

Energy suppliers typically respond within 8 weeks (regulatory requirement)

4

Negotiation & Settlement

Our experienced negotiators work directly with suppliers to secure maximum compensation on your behalf.

Negotiation Strategy:
  • Professional representation
  • Maximum recovery focus
  • Regular client updates
  • Settlement optimization
Timeline:

2-4 weeks

Success Rate:

95% of submitted claims result in financial recovery

5

Payment & Completion

You receive your compensation directly from the energy supplier. We collect our fee only after successful recovery.

Payment Process:
  • Direct payment to your account
  • Full settlement documentation
  • Tax-efficient structuring
  • Future contract advice
Timeline:

5-10 business days

Our Fee:

25-30% of recovered amount (industry standard)

Common Questions About Our Process

Get answers to frequently asked questions about energy commission claims.

If you've used an energy broker or consultant to arrange your business energy supply in the last 6 years, you likely have a claim. Hidden commissions are extremely common, with over 80% of broker-arranged contracts containing undisclosed fees. Our free assessment will determine your specific claim value.

We typically need your recent energy bills, current energy contract, and any correspondence with your broker. Don't worry if you don't have everything - we can help obtain missing documents from your supplier and have legal rights to access contract information on your behalf.

The average process takes 30 days from claim submission to payment. Initial assessment takes 24-48 hours, legal preparation takes 3-5 days, formal submission is immediate, and negotiations typically conclude within 2-4 weeks. Payment follows within 5-10 business days of settlement.

There are absolutely no upfront costs or hidden fees. We operate on a "no win, no fee" basis, meaning we only get paid when you receive your compensation. Our fee is 25-30% of the recovered amount, which is the industry standard and only charged upon successful recovery.

No, pursuing a commission claim will not affect your current energy supply or relationship with your supplier. This is a separate financial matter regarding past undisclosed commissions. Your energy supply will continue uninterrupted, and most suppliers handle these claims through dedicated departments to avoid any service impact.
