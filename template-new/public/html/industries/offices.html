Why Offices are Targeted

Office buildings have characteristics that make them attractive targets for undisclosed broker commissions.

Modern offices consume substantial electricity for computers, servers, networking equipment, and other IT infrastructure.

Climate Control

Offices require consistent heating, cooling, and ventilation to maintain comfortable working conditions for employees.

Extended Hours
