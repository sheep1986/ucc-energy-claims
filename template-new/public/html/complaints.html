Our Commitment to Excellence

At Utility Commission Claims, we strive to deliver outstanding service at every stage of your claim. We take all feedback seriously and are committed to continuous improvement.

Client Focused

Timely Response

Fair Resolution

Our Complaints Process

We've designed our complaints procedure to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring your concerns are addressed efficiently.

1

Submit Your Complaint

Contact us via phone, email, or our online form with details of your concern.

2

Acknowledgment

We'll acknowledge your complaint within 1 business day and assign a dedicated case handler.

3

Investigation

We thoroughly investigate your complaint, reviewing all relevant documentation and communications.

4

Resolution

We provide a written response within 8 weeks, outlining our findings and proposed resolution.

Information to Include in Your Complaint

To help us investigate your complaint efficiently, please include the following information:

  • Your full name and contact details
  • Your UCC claim reference number
  • Date(s) of the incident or issue
  • Details of your complaint
  • What went wrong
  • How it affected you
  • What resolution you're seeking
  • Any supporting documentation

If You're Not Satisfied

We aim to resolve all complaints to your satisfaction. However, if you remain unhappy with our response, you have the right to escalate your complaint to an independent ombudsman.

Legal Ombudsman

For legal services complaints, you can contact the Legal Ombudsman:

  • Website: www.legalombudsman.org.uk
  • Phone: 0300 555 0333
  • Email: enquiries@legalombudsman.org.uk
  • Address: PO Box 6806, Wolverhampton WV1 9WJ

Time Limits: You must contact the Legal Ombudsman within 6 months of our final response to your complaint, or within 6 years of the issue occurring (or 3 years from when you became aware of it).
